Who We Are

Interior scopes is a Lucknow based interior decoration firm. Here we cover all the aspects of interiors, including offices, modular kitchens, residentials, shops, banquets and what not. Our aim is to provide you the best services and assistance in your customised budgets. We believe that everyone's idea of their "HOME SWEET HOME" differs from each other and it should differ too. Don't worry, we are here to shape all your imaginations and curate your customised four walls. We have made several homemakers happy, by curating their customised kitchen. We even provide independent workforce for projects and project managers on a call. The company is capable of developing strategies that are suitable for producing high-quality designs. We offer interior and exterior design services, as well as house and building construction. Our team consists of the best designers in the industry, capable of conceptualising projects of any size and volume.


"You build, we decor!"
